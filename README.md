A simple **React/TypeScript frontend** that calculates your pay based on the days you work. Designed to give a quick overview of your earnings and adjust for different rates or extra pay times.  

## Features

- Select working days to see your total pay  
- Adjust your **hourly rate**  
- Configure days and times that give **extra pay**  
- Personalizable for your own work schedule and pay structure
